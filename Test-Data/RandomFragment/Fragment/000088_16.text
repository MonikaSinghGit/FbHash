Walla Walla County is bounded south and east by the Blue Mountains, and west and north by the Columbia and Snake Rivers. Area 1,250 square miles. Value of taxable property, $4,421,000: assessed acreage, 134,861. Population, 8,716. The country is a high rolling prairie, well watered by many rivers and their lateral branches.
